Medcare

Medcare was a group project done with two of my classmates during my studies in UX Design Master course in Maeers MIT university in Pune, India. Basically it's a project done by the aim of focusing on the sprint methodology, from brain stroming to sketching and coming up with a solution in the shortest time possible.

Shiva Kumar was our visiting faculty from Bangalore for three days and our mission was firstly to find a problem and then solve it during a short period of time. For brainstorming we chose a really interesting method to analyze issues easier. Simply we decided to have a two-column table: the first column is different age groups and the second one is the topics. By connecting the cells from left column to the right a new topic is made that you go deep inside to find the real issues and then to decide what to work on and tackle.
