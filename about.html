About Me

I love learning how people think and behave, and I leverage research to design user-centered products and experiences which solve both user and business problems. My process is hands-on, collaborative, and iterative. Although I have a holistic view of user experience design, I am most focused on interaction design, user research, and usability testing.

I attended Computer Engineering as my first college degree becuase I love logics and planning. On the other hand, I always loved arts so I decided to learn something that covers both. I took my Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Design, which helped me develop creative thinking and visual storytelling.

I transitioned to UX design because firstly I had the background in programming and secondly UI and UX are all about PRODUCTS too. I love the moment that I see my product is being used by someone. It used to be a physical product in Industrial Design, and now it's designing a digital product (software).
